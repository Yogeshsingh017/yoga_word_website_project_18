What is yoga?

In terms of practice, Yoga is a discipline that over 300 million people around the world are actively engaged in. The word “Yoga” literally means “union,” and refers to an inner state where one experiences everything as a part of oneself. Often mistaken for a system of physical exercise, the Yogic system is actually a set of tools for self-transformation that are designed to bring one to this state of union. Yogic practices are a science and technology for turning inward. Yoga is a subjective tool to explore the inner dimension of a human being that is beyond the accumulations of body and mind.
